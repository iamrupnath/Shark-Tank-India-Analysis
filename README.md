## ğŸ“Š Key Insights

* Only about **half of the pitches** secure a deal.
* **High ask amounts and inflated valuations** significantly reduce deal success.
* Successful deals often involve **valuation cuts and higher equity dilution**.
* Offering **very low equity does not improve deal chances**.
* **Multi-shark deals** increase funding but reduce capital efficiency for founders.
* Sharks exhibit **distinct investment strategies**, from aggressive to selective.
* The best deals maximize **capital raised per unit of equity**, not deal size alone.
